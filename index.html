// about

Architecting DeFi from the ground up

I design and build blockchain protocol architectures for production — not proofs of concept. My work spans institutional tokenization platforms (RWA), ERC-4626 staking vaults, oracles and data-feed systems, AMM integrations, governance contracts, and cross-chain bridges.

I lead end-to-end delivery: translating ambiguous requirements into contract architecture, defining edge cases and protocol invariants, implementing with Solidity and Foundry/Hardhat, writing exhaustive fork tests, and staying accountable post-deployment.

I leverage a suite of AI models (Claude Code, Codex, Kimi, Grok, Gemini) as design partners during critical architectural phases. Rather than bypassing engineering rigor, I rely on them to stress-test technical trade-offs before implementation. Furthermore, AI agents and skills operate as my day-to-day coding and review assistants, ensuring robust test coverage and proactively catching edge cases.

On the open-source side, I'm the official maintainer of Solhint, one of the most widely adopted Solidity linters. I drive releases, rule design, bug triage, and developer experience improvements across the ecosystem.
